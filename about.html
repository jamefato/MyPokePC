ABOUT

Welcome to MyPokéPC!

MyPokéPC is a free online system meant to both facilitate information distribution of Pokémon, as well as track information of caught Pokémon for avid players and shiny hunters.

This site is built around four main pages, Dex, Search, Target, and Input.

DEX

Here is where you view your recorded Pokémon. All Pokémon that you have caught and recorded will automatically display here for you to view at any time.

All information about each Pokémon can be displayed, including species, nickname, nature, date found, gender, and more.

What's more, this can be shared at any time! As long as you have one recorded Pokémon, you will be able to download a copy of your Pokédex to share with your friends!
